WORLD

KOKODI is an avatar collection of NFTs randomly generated on the Ethereum blockchain created from our love of art, anime, and RPG games. The collection consists of two parts - 5555 unique characters (KokoDi) and unique masks. You will be able to combine your kokodi with any mask you own. This will dynamically change your kokodi NFT and will be reflected visually on Opensea! Each kokodi and mask has its own stats and properties that you can combine to achieve thousands of different builds. This will determine your kokodi's effectiveness in facing the dangers of the KOKODI world in a future game. We went to great lengths to carefully design and generate the kokodi and masks, so that whichever kokodi you choose and whatever mask you put on it, they will look great together.
